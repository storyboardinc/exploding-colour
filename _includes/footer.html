Frequently Asked Questions

How much is it?

$40

Is there a discount for kids?

YES! 12 and under can run for $20 but they will not get a branded t-shirt. They will get sunglasses. All kids wishing to go this route do NOT need to register online. Instead their parent/guaradian will sign them in on race day where they can pay $20 cash then.

What time should I arrive on May 22nd?

Registration opens at 1:30pm with the first set of runners heading out at 2pm.

Do I have to raise money?

Nope. Just sign up and come to have fun!

How does it work?

Beginning at 2pm we'll start sending out groups of 10-15 runners in 5 minute intervals. At each KM marker the runner has coloured powder thrown on them so that by the end of the race they look like a million bucks.

Is the event timed?

No. It's not a race, just a run. Everyone goes at their own pace.

What do I get?

An awesome t-shirt and a rad set of sunglasses. You'll receive those items at registration.

Is the event pet friendly?

We like pets but just not at the event.

Is the event kid friendly?

We like kids and they're welcome to participate if they're 6 and up.

Where do I park?

There will be signs on site with parking instructions.

How long will the route take?

It should take anywhere from 30-45 minutes unless you take your sweet time.

Will there be food on site?

Camp Widjitiwiin will be hosting a BBQ after the event for interested participants.

Should I bring water?

Sure! But we'll also have water stations.

Is the Colour Powder safe?

Our colour powder is completely cosmetic grade - meaning, yes, it's safe.
